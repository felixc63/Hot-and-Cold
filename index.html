Instructions

In the game of Hot and Cold, the player gets a set number of turns to find a number. As the player guesses, (s)he will be informed of how close (s)he is to the actual number in terms of hot and cold.

To get started add or subtract to your current guess by clicking the buttons on the far right. Click commit when you're done!

Refer to the following chart for responses to the guess:

Greater than 55 Very Cold
Within 41-55 Cold
Within 31-40 Very Cool
Within 21-30 Cool
Within 16-20 Warm
Within 9-15 Very Warm
Within 6-8 Hot
Within 5 Very Hot
